Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Gymnio ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and want to be transparent about our data practices.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from our users. Gymnio is designed to work without requiring any personal information from you.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers
  • Health or fitness data
  • Any other personally identifiable information

Data Storage

All data you enter into the app is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Third-Party Services

Gymnio does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal data, our app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
